The Exchange

How does this work?

1) Sign up

2) You register for classes, and optionally, list which classes you took and when

3) You list which books you have from those classes and upload pictures of them

4) We email your professors asking them about what books are being used

5) We use magic and initiate a trade for you.

6) You tell us whether you made the trade. If you did, awesome! If you didn't, we'll pair you up with someone else
