The Donations

1) How does this help?

Well, once a book for a certain class is no longer in circulation (it's out of date), we will notify everyone who has taken the class, as well as everyone who is currently registered for the class notifying them of the book change.

Then, we contact local schools in the area who might be interested in those books and we put you in touch!

We are not endorsed by Carnegie Mellon University. All trades are made at the discretion of the owners
